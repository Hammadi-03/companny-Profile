Qatar Airways

A multiple award-winning airline, Qatar Airways was named the World's Best Airline for the ninth time at the 2025 Skytrax World Airline Awards. The airline also received accolades for its Business Class, Business Class Airline Lounge, and was recognized as the Best Airline in the Middle East for the 13th time. Qatar Airways connects to over 170 destinations through its hub, Hamad International Airport, which was named the Best Airport in the Middle East for the 11th consecutive year.
